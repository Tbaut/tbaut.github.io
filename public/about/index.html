About me

I'm a software engineer passionate about web3, privacy, and.. good UX! I know, there's still a long way to go to make web3 easy to use, but I like challenges! I've been a product manager for several years before becoming a full-stack TS/JS developer.

I believe that the best PMs are those who understand the code of their products, and the best developers those who understand their users and the market in which their software evolves.

I like bouldering, traveling, and photography. You can find out about my code on Github, my writings on Medium, some of my photographs here and contact me using any of the following links.
